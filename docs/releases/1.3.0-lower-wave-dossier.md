# MIR 1.3.0 Lower-Wave Release Dossier

MIR 1.3.0 is a canonical MIR 3.1.9-derived target projection for Factorio 0.13 from anchor `7ca81bbc98f02cfaf2d40012096d94e261e74d98`. It is feature-complete for positively supported target capabilities, not identical to MIR 3.1.9, not full modern parity, and not a native-infinite parity claim.

## Scope, Retention, And Limitations

The package implements target-supported finite continuation ladders, stable identities, target-era science and technology fields, target-era settings and locale, iterative graph safety, disabled-science handling, conditional weapon ownership where proven, fixed finite counts, and `global` state. Unsupported native-infinite fields, unproven modifiers, recipe productivity, Space Age, quality, recycler, cargo-only effects, modern caps, settings profiles, and unsupported scripted effects are omitted. No legitimate same-line predecessor exists, so qualification proves first-release create/reload and retention without inventing an upgrade. Manual visual, tree-layout, locale-fit, and human balance review remain `PENDING-MAINTAINER`.
