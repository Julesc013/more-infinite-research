# MIR 2.4.1 Validation Summary

## Automated Result

The earlier six-scenario 2.4.1 startup-hotfix candidate is superseded. Its archive and seal must not be published because it did not prove complete parity with the final MIR 3.1.9 portable behavior set.

The new regression disables the vanilla `automation-science-pack` technology, explicitly leaves the corresponding recipe initially available, and then requires every MIR-generated technology to retain an enabled, acyclic, reachable prerequisite graph. This reproduces the reported 2.3.5 configuration without weakening MIR's strict terminal graph assertion.

The replacement is a fresh Factorio 2.0 projection of sealed MIR 3.1.9 commit `9c8f40038e42fda378b855bb8132fd98a180b6a5` and canonical package-source commit `79df29b50ea9b855b8665d6c9a3d8295806acde0`. `.mir/backport-source-lock.json` enumerates the target-specific package adaptations, while published MIR 2.4.0 remains the exact save-upgrade predecessor.

Qualification is in progress. This record will be filled from the final structured evidence and immutable seal only after focused changed-feature checks and one complete Factorio 2.0 qualification pass succeed.

## Remaining Boundary

MIR 2.4.1 is not release-ready until the replacement candidate is qualified and sealed. Interactive review, tagging, GitHub release creation, and Mod Portal upload remain maintainer-only gates and are not performed during this preparation run.
