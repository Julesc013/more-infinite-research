# MIR 2.5.0 Persistent Verification Backport

MIR 2.5.0 projects the MIR 3.2.0 content-addressed verification system onto Factorio 2.0. It also carries the target-capability guard that prevents unsupported `mod-data` prototypes from being emitted on the 2.0 engine. Public setting IDs, generated technology IDs, migrations, and runtime-state namespaces remain stable from MIR 2.4.5.

## Target Contract

`validation/tests.yml` owns stable test and scenario instance IDs. `validation/domains.yml` owns package-domain classification. `validation/profiles/factorio-2.0.json` binds the Factorio 2.0 deterministic seed, evidence lifetime, exact 2.4.5 upgrade source, and `assert-upgrade-2-4-5-to-2-5-0` fixture.

Every target plan assigns `REUSE`, `WAIT`, `RUN`, or `INVALID`. Evidence from Factorio 2.1 cannot satisfy the 2.0 gate because the fingerprint includes the target, candidate domains or exact archive, Factorio binary, scenario record, fixtures, settings, dependency contract, and verification profile.

## Package Domains

Factorio gameplay scenarios depend on declared data, settings, runtime, migration, balance, locale, asset, dependency, or unknown domains. Deterministic packaging and exact-ZIP loading remain bound to the exact 2.5.0 archive. Upgrade proof remains bound to the exact 2.4.5 and 2.5.0 archives.

This candidate is not version-only: the `mod-data` capability guard changes the Factorio 2.0 data domain and requires fresh gameplay qualification. Later metadata-only candidates may reuse gameplay evidence only when every declared gameplay domain is unchanged.

## Acceptance

- Static validation and verifier self-tests pass on `tmp/2.0`.
- The exact Factorio 2.0 candidate loads through isolated scenario workers.
- An unchanged replan produces exact trusted `REUSE`.
- The aggregate `MIR / verification-gate` requires evidence for every planned fingerprint.
- The exact MIR 2.4.5 archive upgrades to the exact MIR 2.5.0 archive with native-owner settings, technology level, current research, fractional progress, and fixture state retained.
- `.mir/backport-source-lock.json` binds the target projection to Dev commit `bd828d1d21a1038abbc9f0ecbf2c1036e744180d`.
