# MIR 1.5.0 Lower-Wave Release Dossier

MIR 1.5.0 is a canonical MIR 3.1.9-derived target projection for Factorio 0.15 from anchor `7ca81bbc98f02cfaf2d40012096d94e261e74d98`. It is feature-complete for positively supported target capabilities, not identical to MIR 3.1.9 and not full modern parity.

## Scope, Retention, And Limitations

The package implements the minimal binary-proven native-infinite direct-effect surface, target-era science and technology fields, target-era settings and locale, iterative graph safety, disabled-science handling, conditional weapon ownership where proven, compact formulas, and `global` state. Unsupported modifiers, recipe productivity, Space Age, quality, recycler, cargo-only effects, modern caps, settings profiles, and unsupported scripted effects are omitted. No legitimate same-line predecessor exists, so qualification proves first-release create/reload and retention without inventing an upgrade. Manual visual, tree-layout, locale-fit, and human balance review remain `PENDING-MAINTAINER`.

## Feature, Settings, Locale, And Balance References

Nine fallback-aligned locale catalogs undergo structural and target-load checks. `.mir/evidence/lower-wave/1.5.0-balance-summary.json` records deterministic costs, effects, bounds, science reachability, and ownership checks.
