## Defect And Correction

The C10 discovery pipeline used a Lua pseudo-ternary whose true branch was `nil`. In Lua, `condition and nil or fallback` always selects the fallback, so even a single exact CompatibilityPack item candidate received an ambiguity record. That suppressed an intended reviewed attachment and caused `scenario/2.1/semantic-family-attach` to fail after 18 preceding fresh C10 lanes had passed.

C11 uses an explicit conditional: an ambiguity record exists only when the candidate item count is not exactly one. The regression contract now proves both sides:

- one exact candidate seed is preserved without ambiguity;
- a seed with neither an exact item nor one inferable item remains `ambiguous_candidate_seed`.

The semantic-family fixture also declares its six intended positive recipes through exact reviewed CompatibilityPack includes. This keeps structurally inferred high-progression candidates subject to the C10 review budgets while making the fixture's intended ownership authority explicit. Hard-risk candidates and the deliberately unreviewed control remain non-emitting.
