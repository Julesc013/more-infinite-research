# MIR 1.9.4 Validation Summary

MIR 1.9.4 is an unreleased Factorio 1.1 maintenance candidate. It keeps the 1.9.3 reduced compatibility contract and adds the portable safety and release-verification improvements proven in MIR 3.1.5 and 2.4.0. Unsupported 2.x behavior remains an explicit target cut.

## Proven Before Packaging

- The complete static gate passes.
- The exact Factorio 1.1.110 binary loads the package and all reduced runtime scenarios.
- Generated prerequisite validation rejects disabled, cyclic, self-locked, and missing-mechanism research paths.
- Generated prerequisite validation walks deep modded graphs iteratively while retaining deterministic cycle diagnostics.
- External infinite weapon-speed continuations remain authoritative and unmodified.
- Recipe productivity, DLC, cargo, prototype-limit, recycler, unrestricted-module, and settings-profile surfaces remain excluded.
- Settings for every omitted recipe-productivity stream, including low density structure, plastic, processing unit, and rocket fuel, are omitted so Factorio 1.1 players are not shown inert controls.
- The package is independently byte-reproducible and the complete runtime lane consumes the exact release-candidate archive.
- Fresh create/reload and the 1.9.3 upgrade path are rerun against the final bytes before sealing.
