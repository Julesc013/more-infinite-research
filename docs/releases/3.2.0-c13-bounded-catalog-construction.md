## Performance Finding

The clean exact-archive probe compares the sealed 3.1.9 archive with C13 on the same Factorio 2.1.11 installation. One smoke pair observed approximately 4.18 seconds for 3.1.9 and 15.84 seconds for C13. The instrumented C13 compiler planning boundary was approximately 9.43 seconds; a temporary non-shipping detail pass attributed approximately 3.56 seconds to final TechnologyCatalog construction and 2.36 seconds to the pure compiler, with snapshot capture and qualification remaining bounded. C13's telemetry stays below the separate 20-second compiler-total counter limit, but the paired total-load and planning regression policy is stricter and remains unsatisfied.

The release authorities therefore record performance as blocked. The gate has not been weakened, the smoke result is not promoted as passing release evidence, and the temporary detail hooks were removed. A complete paired campaign, final automated qualification, manual attestation, protected qualification, seal, tag, and publication remain pending until the performance policy passes or an explicitly reviewed future release contract supersedes it.
