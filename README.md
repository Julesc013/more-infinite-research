Trickle down economics bring productivity gains to all industries!

More Infinite Research adds configurable infinite productivity and bonus researches for intermediate items, logistics chains, combat bonuses, player bonuses, and Space Age gaps that vanilla does not cover.

The mod targets Factorio 2.1.8+ and generates its technologies late in the prototype stage so it can see more recipes, labs, science packs, and technology changes from other mods. Science packs are discovered from active lab inputs instead of from a hard-coded prototype type, which keeps the generated technologies compatible with Factorio 2.1.7+ science-pack changes and many modded science-pack setups.

Recipe productivity researches are intentionally infinite, but Factorio's maximum productivity cap still applies. Additional levels can eventually have no practical effect once a recipe reaches its cap.

Each generated technology can be individually disabled, cost curves can be tuned per stream, and vanilla weapon shooting speed edits are setting-controlled.

Space Age also gets an infinite cargo bay unloading distance research for the 2.1 landing pad unloading bay. It requires the vanilla unloading bay unlock, uses all available science packs by default, and adds 10 tiles of cargo landing pad unloading distance per level. The default cost curve starts at 100,000 research units and triples each level, but the enable switch, max level, base cost, growth factor, and research time are startup settings like the other generated streams.

This mod was created to make megabasing easier on lower-end hardware while staying compatible with broad modded playthroughs. Unknown mods that mutate recipes or labs later than this mod in `data-final-fixes.lua` may still need explicit load-order compatibility.
