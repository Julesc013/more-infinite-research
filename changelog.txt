Version: 2.1.0
Date: 2026-07-03

  Features:

    - Spoilage Preservation now uses agricultural, cryogenic, and space science.
    - Agricultural Growth Speed now uses agricultural, electromagnetic, and cryogenic science.
    - Rocket Shooting Speed and Cannon Shooting Speed now use electromagnetic science.
    - Cargo Bay Unloading Distance now uses the landing pad unloading bay technology icon.
    - Cargo Landing Pad Count now uses the Space platform technology icon.
    - Added fluid-output productivity for oil processing, cracking, lubricant, sulfuric acid, acid neutralization, and thrusters.
    - Fluid productivity now uses cryogenic oil, agricultural cracking, electromagnetic lubricant, and metallurgic acid science.
    - Oil Cracking productivity uses Oil processing art, and Sulfuric Acid productivity uses sulfuric acid fluid art.
    - Rail Productivity covers Elevated Rails supports and ramps, using Elevated Rails technology art when active.
    - Split Stone Product Productivity into Landfill, Artificial Soil, and Molten Metals productivity.
    - Landfill Productivity gives +10% landfill and +5% foundation productivity, using landfill technology art.
    - Artificial Soil Productivity gives +10% artificial soil and +5% overgrowth soil productivity, with space science.
    - Molten Metals Productivity gives +10% lava metals and +5% ore melting productivity, using Foundry art.
    - Lithium Productivity gives +5% productivity to lithium from brine when the Space Age recipe exists.
    - Added Carbon Productivity for asteroid carbon recipes, burnt spoilage at +5%, and coal synthesis at +2%.
    - Added Ice Productivity for oxide asteroid crushing and compatible ice-output recipes.
    - Added Bacteria Cultivation Productivity for iron and copper bacteria cultivation recipes.
    - Bacteria Cultivation and Breeding Productivity now include cryogenic science.
    - Added an opt-in pipeline extent startup multiplier; the default 100% leaves prototypes unchanged.

  Compatibility:

    - Vanilla Space Age productivity techs can adopt safe residual recipes for processing units, plastic, LDS, and rocket fuel.
    - Existing saves reset technology effects when adopted vanilla-family recipe signatures change.
    - Known Plates n Circuit Productivity techs are replaced only when MIR fully matches recipes and effect values.
    - Known competitor cleanup leaves partial, mismatched, or externally blocked technologies in place.
    - Unknown external recipe-productivity owners make MIR suppress matching effects instead of duplicating ownership.
    - Elevated Rails and Quality remain hidden load-order dependencies without separate visible requirements.

  Migration:

    - Retired Stone Product Productivity progress migrates into Landfill Productivity.
    - Artificial Soil Productivity and Molten Metals Productivity start as new separate research lines.

    - The late-game science gate is now disabled by default instead of forcing space or promethium science.
    - Added science-pack ingredient policies for configured, space, promethium, official, or active lab packs.
    - Space Age cargo logistics research now uses official base and Space Age science packs only.
    - Space Age cargo logistics streams now skip cleanly when Space Age is disabled.

    - Optional DLC-shaped streams no longer hard-require Space Age.

    - Recipe matching skips hidden and recycling recipes unless a stream opts in.
    - Breeding Productivity matching no longer catches every biochamber recipe.
    - Base-tech extensions inherit science packs from the last vanilla level and add only the intended extra packs.

    - Equivalent existing infinite base-tech continuations are skipped instead of duplicated.

    - Base-extension zero values now derive from the vanilla chain instead of MIR defaults.
    - Positive max-level settings below the first generated continuation now skip that base extension.
    - Cargo Landing Pad Count is tied to the vanilla cargo landing pad unlock.
    - Compatibility profiles can append stream items, patterns, exclusions, and groups without replacing arrays.

  Compatibility:

    - Raised the base dependency to Factorio 2.1.8 or newer.
    - Cargo Bay Unloading Distance uses the Factorio 2.1.8 technology modifier API.
    - Added optional official DLC ordering floors for Elevated Rails, Recycler, Quality, and Space Age.
    - No compatibility-mod dependencies are declared; integrations use visible prototypes and safe skip checks.
    - Added competing base-extension cleanup for compatible infinite continuations.
    - Better Robots Extended worker robot storage research can be replaced by MIR when the matching extension is enabled.
    - Known recipe-productivity cleanup removes infinite competitors only after replacement MIR effects exist.
    - Finite upgrade chains from other mods are not removed by the generic cleanup path.
    - Compatibility cleanup removes dangling prerequisite references when deleting known competing technologies.
    - Worker Robot Storage research now leans toward utility and electromagnetic science in Space Age.
    - Added opportunistic compatibility for Advanced Solar HR, OCs Ammo and Armor, OCs Stone Casting, and Plates n Circuit.
    - Added opportunistic compatibility for Fluid Quality Imprinting.
    - OCs Ammo and Armor biochamber explosives recipes are matched explicitly.
    - Turbo belt, underground belt, and splitter productivity are covered explicitly at the 1% tier in Space Age.
    - Rocket Shooting Speed now uses base-game rocketry art instead of Space Age rocket turret art.

Version: 1.0.0
Date: 2025-10-25

  Features:

    - Initial public release for Factorio 2.0 with optional Space Age support.
    - Added generated infinite recipe productivity technologies for many intermediate items and resources.
    - Generated productivity technologies cover resources not covered by vanilla productivity research.
    - Added per-technology startup enable switches for every generated research stream.
    - Added automatic recipe scanning for covered outputs and generation of matching productivity effects.
    - Added conflict trimming so recipes already covered by existing productivity technologies are skipped.
    - Added Space Age-aware science-pack selection and prerequisites for generated technologies.
    - Added a default infinite research cost curve of `8000 * 2^(L-1)` with 60-second research units.

  Initial research coverage:

    - Added productivity streams for breeding, plastic, sulfur, batteries, explosives, and gears.
    - Added productivity streams for iron sticks, copper cable, circuits, processing units, and low density structures.
    - Added productivity streams for rocket fuel, plates, engine units, electric engines, and flying robot frames.
    - Added Space Age and overhaul-oriented streams for tungsten, holmium, supercapacitors, and superconductors.
    - Added Space Age and overhaul-oriented streams for bioflux, carbon fiber, lithium, and quantum processors.
    - Added grouped productivity streams for modules, belts, inserters, bullets, and rockets.
    - Grouped productivity streams use lower per-level gains for higher-tier items inside those groups.

  Notes:

    - The technologies are intentionally infinite, but Factorio's recipe productivity cap still applies.
    - The mod was created to make megabasing easier on lower-end hardware.
    - The mod still preserves Factorio's normal production and logistics constraints.
